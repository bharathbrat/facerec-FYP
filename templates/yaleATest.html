Design and Analysis of Face Recognition Algorithms

The tests available on Yale Database A are:

  • General test - First 6 images (i.e 'centerlight','glasses','happy','leftlight','noglasses','normal') are put into training dataset and the next 5 images (i.e. 'rightlight','sad','sleepy','surprised','wink') into testing dataset.
  • Illumination test - The images that have no variation in pose, expression and illumination (i.e 'glasses','noglasses','normal') are put into training dataset while images with illumination variation (i.e. 'centerlight','leftlight','rightlight') are put into testing dataset.
  • Expression test - Normal images (i.e. 'noglasses','normal') are put into training dataset and images with variation in expression (i.e. 'happy','sleepy','sad','wink','surprised') are put into testing dataset.
  • Single Image test - This test gives the user the choice to select an image from the testing dataset (i.e. 'centerlight','glasses','happy','leftlight','noglasses'), which will be compared with other images in training dataset(i.e. 'normal','rightlight','sad','sleepy','surprised','wink') to give a success or failure.
